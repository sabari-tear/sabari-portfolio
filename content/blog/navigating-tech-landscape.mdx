## My Personal Journey and Insights

I feel incredibly fortunate to have begun my software engineering journey at a time when JavaScript frameworks were not as advanced as they are now. This allowed me to build a strong foundation in the basics: vanilla JavaScript, jQuery, plain CSS, and languages like PHP and SQL.

While modern frameworks accelerate development, true adaptability comes from understanding the fundamentals. They empower engineers to switch stacks, solve edge-cases, and think beyond library constraints.

## Conclusion

Becoming a software engineer first, rather than a framework specialist, builds resilience for long-term growth in a rapidly changing industry.
